# Delivery Policy – Logistic Journey

At **LogisticJourney.com**, we are committed to **Making Every Journey Count** by providing a reliable and efficient journey and fleet management platform. This Delivery Policy outlines how our services—and where applicable, products—are provisioned, activated, and maintained for our clients.

---

## 1. Scope of Delivery

This policy applies to the delivery of:

- **Software Services** (platform access and functionality)
- **Driver Mobile App**
- **System Implementation & Onboarding**

---

## 2. Service Delivery (SaaS)

### a. Platform Access

Upon successful subscription and payment confirmation:

- Customers will receive access credentials via email within **24–48 hours**.
- Access includes the **Admin Portal**, **Driver Mobile App**, and any relevant **API integrations**.

### b. Onboarding & Setup

For enterprise clients, we offer assisted onboarding, which includes:

- Organizational setup
- Role-based access configuration
- Driver and vehicle data imports

Setup is typically completed within **5 business days**, subject to client responsiveness and data availability.

---

## 3. Mobile App Delivery

- The **Driver App** is available on both **Android** and **iOS** platforms.
- Download links and user guides are provided during onboarding.

---

## 4. Delivery Delays

While we strive to meet all delivery timeframes, delays may occur due to:

- Incomplete or inaccurate client information
- Technical issues beyond our control
- Unforeseen operational constraints

We will communicate any delays promptly and work to resolve them efficiently.

---

## 5. Amendments to This Policy

We reserve the right to update this policy periodically. Any changes will be:

- Published on our website
- Communicated via email to active customers
