# Cancellation Policy – Logistic Journey

At **Logistic Journey**, we aim to offer flexibility and clarity around service cancellations. This policy outlines how and when cancellations can be made, in accordance with South Africa’s **Consumer Protection Act (CPA)**.

---

## 1. Free Setup

We offer a **free organization setup**, including:
- Company profiles
- Driver and vehicle registration

This ensures you're fully set up and satisfied before billing begins.

---

## 2. Monthly Billing

- All subscriptions are billed **monthly per vehicle**.
- Cancellations made **before the billing period begins** will not incur charges.
- Once the billing cycle starts, the subscription remains active until the end of the cycle.

---

## 3. General Cancellations

- You may cancel your subscription **at any time**.
- Access to services will remain active until the end of the current billing cycle.
- **Mid-cycle cancellations** do **not qualify for partial refunds**.

---

## 4. Training, Consulting, or Workshop Bookings

- **Cancellations ≥ 7 Days Before Event**: Full refund or rescheduling allowed.
- **Cancellations < 7 Days Before Event**: 50% cancellation fee applies.
- **No-shows**: No refund will be issued.

---

## 5. Termination by Logistic Journey

We reserve the right to terminate services under the following conditions:

- Violation of our [Terms of Service](#)
- Non-payment of invoices
- Abuse or misuse of the platform or staff

In such cases, **no refunds** will be issued.

---

## 6. How to Cancel

To cancel your subscription or scheduled service, please email **support@logisticjourney.com** with the following details:

- Your company name  
- The service or subscription to cancel  
- Reason for cancellation
