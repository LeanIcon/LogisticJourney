# Refund & Cancellation Policy – Logistic Journey

At **Logistic Journey**, we are committed to providing reliable and high-quality logistics solutions. This Refund & Cancellation Policy outlines the conditions under which refunds may be granted, in accordance with South African consumer protection laws.

---

## 1. Eligibility for Refunds

You may be eligible for a full or partial refund under the following circumstances:

- **Billing Errors**: If you report a billing error within **7 calendar days** of the transaction.
- **Subscription Cancellation**: If you cancel your subscription **before the start of the new billing cycle**.
- **Service Disruption**: If there is a **continuous service outage exceeding 48 hours**, and the issue is not caused by user-side factors.
- **Defective or Misrepresented Services**: If the service provided does not meet the agreed-upon standards or differs materially from what was advertised, as per Section 55 and 56 of the CPA.

---

## 2. Non-Refundable Situations

Refunds will not be issued in the following cases:

- **Partial Usage**: If the service was partially used during the billing cycle.
- **Unused Features**: If drivers, journeys, or dispatchers included in your plan remain unused.
- **Late Cancellation**: If cancellation is requested **after the billing cycle has commenced**.
- **Change of Mind**: Refunds will not be granted for cancellations due to a change of mind, unless covered under direct marketing provisions.

---

## 3. Subscription Cancellation Terms

In accordance with **Section 14 of the CPA**, fixed-term contracts may be cancelled by the subscriber with **20 business days’ written notice**. Early cancellation may incur a **reasonable cancellation fee**, calculated based on the remaining term and usage.

---

## 4. Refund Request Procedure

To request a refund, please contact our support team at **support@logisticjourney.com** with the following details:

- Your company name  
- Date and reference of the transaction  
- Reason for the refund request  

We aim to process all valid refund requests within **5 business days** of receipt.

---

## 5. Direct Marketing & E-Commerce Transactions

If you subscribed through **direct marketing**, you are entitled to cancel the agreement within **5 business days** of receiving the service, as per Section 16 of the CPA. For online purchases, Section 44 of the ECTA allows cancellation within **7 days of receipt**, provided the service has not been used.

---

## 6. Modifications to This Policy

We reserve the right to update or modify this policy at any time. Significant changes will be communicated via email or dashboard notifications. Continued use of our services after such changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
