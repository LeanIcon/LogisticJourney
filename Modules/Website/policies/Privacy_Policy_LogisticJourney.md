# Privacy Policy – Logistic Journey

At **Logistic Journey**, protecting your personal information is a top priority. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, and safeguard your data in accordance with South Africa’s **Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA)**.

This policy applies to our websites and services, including:
- [www.LogisticJourney.com](https://www.logisticjourney.com)
- [app.LogisticJourney.com](https://app.logisticjourney.com)

By accessing or using our services, you consent to the practices described below.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Personal Information
We may collect personal details such as:
- Name
- Address
- Phone number
- Email address

This information is used to facilitate logistics operations, manage accounts, and communicate with you.

### Driver Location Data
If you use the **Logistic Journey Driver App**, we collect **real-time location data** (including background location) to:
- Track deliveries
- Manage geofencing
- Optimize routing

Location data is collected **only when you are marked as "online" or "on shift"**. We do **not** collect or store location data when you are offline.

### Technical & Usage Data
We automatically collect:
- IP address
- Browser type
- Operating system
- Access times
- Referring URLs

This data helps us improve service quality and user experience.
